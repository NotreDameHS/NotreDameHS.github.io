My Game is a topdown Metroidvania style game where the player travels around the map defeating enemies and collecting powerups.  The goal of the game is to defeat the 
onslaught of enemies in the labratory area unlocking the door and collecting the White Crystal.  The player can control the characters movement with the W/A/S/D Keys, Attack
in each direction with its respective arrow Key, as well as preform a dodge roll with the space bar.
