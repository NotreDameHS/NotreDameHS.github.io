Description

A simple top-down-game where your goal is make it to the top of the map where the tents are to win.
You must avoid rocks because they might make you slow down, and slimes that are trying to kill you or you can attack the slimes.
You have 5 Lives before you die 


Controls 

W - Move up
A - Move left
S - Move down
D - Move right

left Click - Attack
